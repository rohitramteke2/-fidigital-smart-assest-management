How It Works

Up and running
in three steps

No complex setup. No expensive hardware. Just scan, track, and manage — from day one.

01
Register Your Assets

Add assets with name, category, serial number, photos, and assign them to team members. Import via CSV or add one by one.

02
Generate & Print QR Codes

Each asset gets a unique QR code and barcode instantly. Download in bulk, print label sheets, and stick them on every device.

03
Scan from Anywhere

Point any phone camera at a QR code for instant identification — no app install needed. Get owner, status, and full history in seconds.
